# Evolutionary Computing Optimization

These laboratory assigements were completed as part of the *Evolutionary Computing Optimization* course at college. The laboratory assigements are implemented in Java.

## First laboratory assigement:  
Solving 3-SAT problem with exhaustive search algorithm, iterative search algorithm, variant of iterative search algorithm, GSAT algorithm, RandomWalkSAT algorithm, and variant with Iterated Local Search.  

Example of usage:  
`java -cp . hr.fer.zemris.trisat.TriSatSolver 1 uf20-010.cnf`

## Second laboratory assigement: 
Numerical optimization using gradient descent to solve various problems involving scalar functions over n-dimensional real-valued vectors.

Example of usage:  
`java -cp . hr.fer.zemris.optjava.dz2.Jednostavno 1 10`  
`java -cp . hr.fer.zemris.optjava.dz2.Prijenosna 10 prijenosna.txt`  
`java -cp . hr.fer.zemris.optjava.dz2.Sustav 10 sustav.txt`  

## Third laboratory assigement: 
Simulated Annealing.  

Example of usage:  
`java -cp . fer.oer.RegresijaSustava sustav.txt` 

## Fourth laboratory assigement: 
Genetic Programming. 

Example of usage:  
`java -cp . oer_dz4.Zadatak f1.txt` 

## Fifth laboratory assigement: 
Clone Selection Algorithm.  

Example of usage:  
`java -cp . oer.dz.App sustav.txt` 

*Note: Example files are provided in examples folder in each laboratory assigement.*
